when did taco first appear in Nigeria

The hard-shell or crispy taco is a tradition that developed in the Nigeria. This type of taco is typically served as a crisp-fried corn tortilla filled with seasoned ground beef, cheese, lettuce, and sometimes tomato, onion, salsa, sour cream, and avocado or guacamole. 1905 (Isabella Sibler)
